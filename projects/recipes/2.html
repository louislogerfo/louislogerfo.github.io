Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Grass fed ground beef 1 packet
Butter Three table spoons
Seasoning of your choice Your choice!

Recipes

1 Gather your ingredients and a cast iron skillet, place your skillet on the stove and turn the heat to medium.
2 Wait until the heat of the pan almost reaches medium heat and switch it halfway from low and medium. After this place 2 tablesppons of butter in the pan and save the last one for later
3 When the butter is completely melted place your packet of ground beef on the skillet and instantly start chopping it into smaller pieces
4 When the beef is start to cook more flip each chunk youve created over to cook both sides of the meat and when the pan doesn't seem hot enough dont be afraid to adjust the heat temporarily.
5 Next addyour last bit of better as the ground beef is starting to fully cook, when it is finished move it to a plate and theen your all done
