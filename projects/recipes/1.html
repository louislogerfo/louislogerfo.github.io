"Most people will add milk to their eggs, but the secret is to add water" - my grandfather

Ingredients

Ingredients Quanitty
Eggs 4-6
Butter 2 Tablespoons
Water Half a cup

Recipe

Order Instructions
1 Gather your ingredients and a cast iron skillet, place your skillet on a stove and turn the heat to medium
2 Pour eggs in a bow land stir them until their scrambled.
3 Wait until the heat of the pan almost reaches medium heat and switch it halfway from low and medium. After this place your butter in the pan
4 When the butter is copmletely melted place the eggs in and start stirring them constantly with a spatula
5 When the eggs are half way cooked, pour the water slowly in the eggs and stir while doing doing so
6 Keep on stiring and scrambling eggs until they are a light fluffy texture, try not to over cook them. When eggs are ready move them from the pan to a plate
